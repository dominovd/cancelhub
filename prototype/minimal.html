How to cancel Netflix

Three taps from any device. No phone call, no chat agent, no retention page. The cancellation takes effect at the end of your current billing cycle and you keep access until then.

Difficulty    easy Price  $6.99 – $24.99/mo Refund  No partial refunds Verified May 8, 2026
Web iOS Android TV
  1. 01

    Open netflix.com/account in any browser and sign in.

  2. 02

    Under Membership & Billing, click Cancel Membership.

  3. 03

    Confirm with Finish Cancellation. You'll see a confirmation screen and receive an email within a minute.

    Netflix will offer to pause your account for up to 3 months — you can decline and finish cancelling.

Heads up

Netflix doesn't issue partial refunds for the current billing period. Cancel at least one day before your renewal date to avoid the next charge. Watch history is preserved for 10 months in case you return.

Common issues

I cancelled but I'm still being charged.

Your subscription may be billed through a third party — Apple, Google Play, T-Mobile, Verizon, or your cable provider. Cancellation has to happen wherever you originally subscribed. Check your billing receipts to find the source.

My account was on a free trial — will I be charged?

Netflix no longer offers free trials in most countries. If you're on a promotional plan, cancellation prevents the next charge but doesn't reverse anything already paid.
